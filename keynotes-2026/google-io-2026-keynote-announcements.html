Chapter 1 — Opening (infrastructure, conversational AI, voice)
1 TPU 8t (training) and TPU 8i (inference) Eighth generation of Google's custom AI silicon, now split into two specialised dies. 8t targets large-scale pre-training and is ~3× the raw compute of the previous generation. 8i targets inference latency, with a live demo of an upcoming Gemini Flash hitting ~1,500 tokens/sec. Both chips deliver up to 2× better performance-per-watt. JAX + Pathways now scale training across multiple data centres and over 1 million TPUs globally. Announced at Cloud Next; recapped here Google Cloud (global) Google Cloud customers
2 Ask YouTube Conversational query layer over YouTube. Returns digestible overviews, the most relevant videos for your context, jumps directly to the relevant part of a video, and remembers context for follow-up questions (with comparison tables when useful). Testing now; broad rollout this summer United States Free (YouTube)
3 Docs Live Voice-driven Doc creation in Google Docs — verbally brain-dump intent, pull in source material from Drive and Gmail, and let Gemini draft and iterate the document. Demoed creating a talk outline in real time without typing. Rolling out this summer; same voice capabilities coming to Gmail and Google Keep at the same time Not specified in keynote Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers
Chapter 2 — Gemini Omni (world models, provenance)
4 Gemini Omni Flash First model in the new Omni "world model" family that creates anything from any input — starting with video generation and conversational video editing. Steps up simulation of intuitive physics (kinetic energy, gravity) and lets you edit a user-supplied video in natural language. Omni Pro coming soon. Launching today across Google products Wherever Gemini products are available See specific product rows for tier gating (e.g. Gemini app: Plus/Pro/Ultra)
5 SynthID + Content Credentials Verification in Search and Chrome SynthID watermark (already on 100B images/videos and 60,000 years of audio) and Content Credentials provenance check now reachable via Circle to Search and right-click in Chrome. Returns whether content was AI-generated or camera-captured, and whether it was edited with generative AI. Expanding to Search and Chrome Wherever Search + Chrome are available Free
6 New SynthID adopters: OpenAI, Kakao, ElevenLabs Three additional partners join NVIDIA (added last year) in watermarking their AI-generated content with SynthID, pushing toward an industry-wide provenance standard. Announced today Global n/a (partner programme)
7 Gemini 3.5 Flash New "frontier intelligence combined with action" model. Better than 3.1 Pro on almost every benchmark, with a large jump on GDPval (real-world economically valuable tasks). Roughly 4× the output speed of other frontier models on the intelligence-vs-speed frontier, at less than half the price of comparable frontier models. Co-optimised with the Antigravity harness. Available today across products and APIs Wherever Gemini products / APIs are available Gemini API + consumer products
8 Gemini 3.5 Pro Higher-capability counterpart to 3.5 Flash; already in internal use at Google with reported large improvements. Coming next month (~June 2026) Not specified in keynote Gemini API + consumer products (tier gating TBC)
Chapter 3 — Google Antigravity
9 Antigravity 2.0 desktop app [also in dev keynote] "Agent-first" desktop application built around the agent conversation, agent-produced artifacts and multi-agent orchestration. Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash co-optimised with the Antigravity Harness. New harness primitives: subagents, hooks, asynchronous task management. Demoed building an entire functional operating system in 12 hours: 93 parallel subagents, 15,000+ model requests, 2.6B tokens, under $1,000 of API credits. Available globally today Global Free; Gemini API usage applies
10 Antigravity CLI [also in dev keynote] Terminal-native counterpart to Antigravity 2.0 with the same harness and models. Rolled out to all Gemini CLI users. Available today Global Free
11 Antigravity SDK [also in dev keynote] Programmatic access to the Gemini-optimised agent harness for deployment on your own infrastructure. Available today Global Free SDK; API charges apply
12 Native voice support in Antigravity (Gemini audio models) Voice-driven interaction with agents inside Antigravity 2.0 and the CLI, using the latest Gemini audio-understanding models. Available today Global Antigravity
13 Subagent teamwork (research preview) Mechanism behind the OS-in-12-hours demo: an agent breaks a complex task into a plan and runs parallel subagents that generate, execute and iterate on their own tests. Early research preview today in Antigravity Global Antigravity
14 12× faster Flash in Antigravity Sundar's 4× speedup of Gemini 3.5 Flash vs other frontier models is extended to 12× inside Antigravity, optimised for the token-heavy nature of agentic coding. Available starting today Global Antigravity

Chapter 5 (cont.) — Agentic Commerce
26 Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) — new partners and verticals Open-source standard for agentic commerce ("HTTP for the agent web of shopping"). New founding partners: Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Salesforce, Stripe. Extending coverage to hotels, local food delivery, YouTube and more products. Announced today; new regions in the coming months Expanding UCP-powered experiences to Canada, Australia, UK in the coming months Open standard
27 Agent Payments Protocol (AP2) Lets an AI agent pay on your behalf within strict, user-set guardrails (brand, product, spend cap). Creates a tamper-proof, privacy-preserving digital mandate linking user / merchant / payment processor for accountable transactions and clean returns. Coming to Google products in the coming months, starting with Gemini Spark Not specified in keynote Initially via Gemini Spark
28 Universal Cart Cross-merchant, cross-service cart on top of Google Wallet. Adds items from Search, Gemini, YouTube or Gmail; tracks price drops, history and stock; surfaces hidden card-perk savings. Checkout via Google Pay or hand-off to the retailer's site. Rolling out this summer in Search and the Gemini app; YouTube and Gmail to follow United States (initial rollout) Free
